Bio

I am currently a senior researcher at Tencent AI Lab. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Ohio State University, where I was advised by Prof. DeLiang Wang. I also hold a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University, where I was supervised by Prof. Jingdong Chen.

My research focuses on artificial intelligence, speech enhancement, speech separation, acoustic echo cancellation, active noise control, acoustic howling suppression, and deep learning. I am also interested in microphone array processing and music source separation

I serve as a reviewer for IEEE/ACM Transactions on Audio, Speech, and Language Processing, Neural Networks, Neurocomputing, Signal Processing, IEEE Signal Processing Letters, The Journal of the Acoustical Society of America, Speech Communication, The Journal of Supercomputing, Journal of Cleaner Production, International Journal of Acoustics and Vibration, Speech Communication, Measurement, Mechanical Systems and Signal Processing, Heliyon, Knowledge-Based Systems, ICASSP, and, INTERSPEECH.
